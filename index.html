Tejashwini

I didn’t plan this feeling.
It grew on its own.

Something changed

I started waiting for your messages.
And smiling without realizing it.

I felt calm

Not excited in a loud way.
But steady. Comfortable. Real.

With you

I don’t need to impress.
I don’t need to hide.
I can just be myself.

This feeling stayed

Through normal days.
Through silence.
Through time.

I choose you

Not because it’s dramatic.
But because it feels right.
