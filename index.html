Tejashwini

I didn’t expect this

There was no plan.
No sudden moment.
It just found its way in.

It grew quietly

In our late-night conversations.
In the way time stopped mattering.
In waiting for your messages.

I felt the change

I became calmer around you.
More honest.
More myself.

With you

Even ordinary days felt special.
Even silence felt safe.
Even nothing felt enough.

I choose you

Not because everything is perfect.
But because it feels right with you.

Will you be my Valentine?
