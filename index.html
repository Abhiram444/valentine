Tejashwini

I didn’t plan this

There was no dramatic beginning.
It just happened.

It grew quietly

In our late-night conversations.
In the way time disappeared.
In waiting for your messages.

I noticed myself changing

Feeling calm around you.
Feeling comfortable being honest.
Feeling at home.

With you

Even ordinary days felt special.
Even silence felt easy.

I choose you

Not because it’s perfect.
But because it feels right.

Will you be my Valentine?
