Tejashwini,

I didn’t fall for you all at once.
It happened slowly — without me noticing.

Somewhere along the way,

I started looking forward to hearing from you.
Even on days that felt heavy.

I noticed the small things.

How calm I felt around you.
How easy it was to be myself.

With you,

I didn’t feel the need to pretend.
I didn’t feel rushed.
I felt understood.

This isn’t a moment.

It’s a feeling that stayed.
And chose to remain.

I choose you.

Not just today.
But in the quiet, ordinary days too.
